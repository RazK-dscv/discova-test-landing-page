How FAM Trip Qualification Works

Our FAM trips are designed for active partners who are committed to growing their Asia programme with Discova. Here's a step-by-step overview of how to earn your place.

1

Reach Your Threshold

Meet the minimum booking or revenue threshold specified for each FAM trip within the qualifying period.

2

Submit Your Interest

Express your interest via the form on this page or reach out directly to your Discova account manager.

3

Receive Confirmation

Our team will review your eligibility and confirm your spot. Spots are limited and awarded on merit.

4

Experience Discova

Immerse yourself in one of Asia's most extraordinary destinations — firsthand, purposeful, and unforgettable.

General Qualification Guidelines

All incentive programmes are open to registered Discova partners. The following process applies across all running programmes.

1

Register as a Partner

Ensure you are a registered Discova trade partner in an eligible market.

2

Book Within the Period

Make qualifying bookings within the specified campaign window using the designated booking process.

3

Track Your Progress

Contact your Discova account manager to track your bookings against each incentive threshold.

4

Claim Your Reward

Rewards are issued after the campaign closes and final booking values are confirmed.
